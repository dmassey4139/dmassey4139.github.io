This site is also designed to serve as a personal site for all of us even after the assignment. We hope to leave this site up on the world wide web so that many can vist and learn more about us!

Our site is intended to give viewers an overview of the biographical information, hobbies, and any special interests of Dakota, Dylan, & Jean. To see the menu, just click on the 3-stripes logo at the top left of your screen and then click it again to hide it. We have also provided a top-of-page link for your convenience (arrow button).
